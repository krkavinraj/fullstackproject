About Agriride

Agriride is a smart transport pooling platform designed specifically for farmers to share or pool transport for their produce. Our mission is to reduce costs, optimize logistics, and ensure timely market delivery for agricultural goods.

Our Vision

We envision a future where everyone in rural communities can travel safely and efficiently, reducing isolation and supporting local economies.

Why Choose Us?

  • Easy ride booking and offering
  • Verified drivers and riders
  • Secure and reliable platform
  • Focus on rural connectivity
